Control your debts. Simple debts manager for storing information about your income and outcome.

Features:
* 3 lists (all / debtors / creditors)
* Long-press on list item for submenu
* Contacts binding
* Transaction history
* Simple light interface
* Sharing CSV-file with history
* Sorting
* Search
* Custom currency
* Ads free
